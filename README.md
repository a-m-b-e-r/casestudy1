Introduction
------------

For Case Study I, Group B performed an analysis based on two datasets, gross domestic product of 190 ranked countries and education data. The dataset comes from the world bank organizationâ€™s website. The two datasets have been combined and merged for the analysis. The data has merged based on the country short code, sorted in ascending order by GDP, and calculated the average GDP rankings for High income: nonOECD, High income: OECD, Low income, Lower middle income, and Upper middle income. For types of analysis performed, the GDP has been plotted and grouped into 5 separate quantile groups.

For the High Income: non-OECD group, the average GDP is $104,249.83 and the average GDP ranking is 58.65.
